# Module 8: NLP & Transformers

**Kailash**: kailash-ml (ModelVisualizer, AutoMLEngine, TrainingPipeline, ModelRegistry, OnnxBridge) | **Scaffolding**: 30%

## Lecture (3h)

- **8A-B** Text Foundations: tokenization (word, BPE, WordPiece), stemming, lemmatization, n-grams, BoW, TF-IDF, BM25
- **8C-D** Embeddings & Sequences: Word2Vec (skip-gram, CBOW), GloVe, ELMo, RNNs, LSTMs, bidirectional models, vanishing gradients in sequences
- **8E-H** Attention & Transformers: scaled dot-product attention, multi-head attention, positional encoding, transformer encoder/decoder, BERT, GPT, transfer learning, NLP tasks, decoding strategies

## Lab (3h) — 8 Exercises

1. Text preprocessing pipeline (tokenization, BPE, stemming, lemmatization, n-grams)
2. Bag of Words and TF-IDF (BoW, TF-IDF formula, BM25, document classification)
3. Word embeddings (Word2Vec skip-gram, analogies, t-SNE visualization)
4. Sequence models — RNNs and LSTMs (gating mechanisms, sentiment analysis)
5. Attention mechanisms (scaled dot-product, multi-head, attention visualization)
6. Transformer architecture (positional encoding, encoder layer, LayerNorm)
7. Transfer learning with AutoMLEngine (transformer fine-tuning, ModelRegistry)
8. Capstone: full NLP pipeline (preprocessing → embeddings → classifier → OnnxBridge)
